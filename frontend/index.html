Trello

Manage your work efficiently with a simple and powerful workflow system. Create organizations, organize boards, and track issues seamlessly — all in one place.

Organizations

Organizations help you group work based on teams, projects, or goals. You can create multiple organizations for different purposes like college, startups, or personal work.

Each organization allows you to manage members and control access. Add teammates and collaborate efficiently within a structured environment.

Think of an organization as your top-level workspace.

Boards

Boards exist inside organizations and help you break work into manageable sections. Each board can represent a project, sprint, or workflow.

Use boards to visually organize tasks and track progress. They act as containers where all your issues (tasks) are managed.

Example: “Website Development”, “Placement Prep”, or “Hackathon Project”.

Issues

Issues are the smallest unit of work — tasks you need to complete. Each issue belongs to a board and moves through different stages.

Track progress using states like: TODO → IN PROGRESS → DONE

This helps you maintain clarity, stay organized, and ensure nothing gets missed.
